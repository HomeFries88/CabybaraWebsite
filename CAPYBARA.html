About Capybaras!

What are they?

They are fuzzy animals that are related to guinea pigs. They have stubby limbs and are the biggest rodents in the world.


What do they like to eat?

Capybaras are herbivores, which means they eat plants. That's about it really...


Lifestyle

Overall, they are pretty chill. They like to just sit down and enjoy life like all things should.
